# Ghost Station Block (GTSB)

**Ghost Station Block** adds detailed railway signal blocks designed for realistic rail infrastructure in Minecraft. The mod focuses on high-quality railway signals that fit perfectly into stations, yards, depots, and large rail networks.

These blocks are ideal for builders who want more authentic railway environments and work great alongside train or transport themed mods.

## Features
- 🚦 Realistic railway signal blocks  
- 💡 Detailed custom models and textures  
- 🏗 Perfect for stations, rail yards, and railway builds  
- 🚆 Designed to fit well with train and transport themed mods
